  • About Us

    • Ristorante conFusion - an institution in Hong Kong for the finest Indian fare, Ristorante conFusion has won accolades for almost 18 years from guests with a discerning taste for traditional Indian cuisine.

    This authentic Indian restaurant with a menu created by renowned Indian chefs direct from their home country, Ristorante conFusion present Indian cuisine in a style that has established it as truly fine dining – Indian style and is recognised as Hong Kong’s best.

    The menu is created to feature traditional Indian classics – exotic kebabs from the Tandoor, starters, mains and all-time favourite traditional Indian desserts are a feature. Classic cocktails and an eclectic choice of wines perfectly match the tingling flavours of Indian cuisine.

    The ever popular Indian buffet lunch is loaded with local delights and represents wonderful value for business lunches or simply lunch with friends.

    Located in the heart of the Central Hong Kong dining precinct, and recognised with Michelin Guide Bibs for many years, Ristorante conFusion clearly offers a slice of India to its passionate guests.
